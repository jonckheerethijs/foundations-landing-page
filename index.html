Some duck facts

Ducks possess specialized waterproof feathers that keep them dry while swimming.

There are over 120 different species of ducks found worldwide, each with its unique characteristics and adaptations.

Many duck species are known for their impressive long-distance migrations.

Ducks communicate through a range of vocalizations, with the classic "quack" being the most well-known.
